01 · The Problem

Clinical case review is scattered and slow

Doctors juggle disconnected tools to document a case, get a second opinion, track outcomes, and stay compliant — with no single source of truth.

🧩

Fragmented tools

Notes, messaging, lab data, and approvals live in separate systems that don't talk to each other.

⏱️

Slow escalation

Urgent or emergency presentations can get buried in a queue instead of reaching the right person immediately.

📋

Weak accountability

Without a full audit trail, it's hard to prove who reviewed what, when, and why a treatment was approved.

02 · The Solution

One platform, from symptom to signed-off treatment

Vianova Health unifies case intake, AI-assisted clinical reasoning, doctor review, team collaboration, and full audit logging into a single, role-aware application.

🩺  For Doctors

Submit a new case in minutes, get an AI-generated confidence-scored assessment, review or edit the recommended treatment, and message colleagues directly — all from one dashboard.

🛡️  For Superadmins

Oversee every case across the organization, assign cases to specific doctors, manage user accounts, and review a complete, tamper-evident activity log.

🔒  Closed Clinical System

Access is restricted to Superadmins and Doctors only — no patient-facing login. Every screen, action, and data point stays inside a controlled clinical environment.

🔔  For Everyone

Targeted email and in-app notifications keep the right people informed the moment something needs their attention — nothing more, nothing less.

03 · Core Workflow

How a case moves through the system

A single, linear path — designed so nothing falls through the cracks.

1
New Case is submitted
A doctor enters patient details, presenting complaint, and clinical history through a guided intake form.
2
AI analysis runs automatically
The clinical engine evaluates the case, assigns a confidence level, flags red flags or emergencies, and drafts a suggested treatment path.
3
Case is routed to the right doctor
A superadmin can assign the case to a specific clinician — who is notified immediately by email and in-app toast.
4
Doctor reviews and decides
The assigned or owning doctor reviews the AI recommendation, edits the treatment if needed, and approves or denies the case.
5
Everything is logged and notified
The outcome is recorded in the audit trail, and only the relevant participants — the case owner and assignee — receive the update.
04 · AI Clinical Engine

Structured, confidence-scored clinical reasoning

Every submitted case is run through an AI-assisted analysis that produces a consistent, structured output — never a free-form guess.

📊 Confidence Levels

Each case is scored High Moderate Low confidence — giving the reviewing doctor an immediate sense of how much scrutiny it needs.

🚨 Red-Flag Detection

The engine actively screens for emergency indicators and marks a case as emergency or requires urgent review so it can never be missed in a routine queue.

💊 Suggested Treatment

A draft treatment plan is generated for the reviewing doctor to accept, edit, or override — the AI assists, the clinician always decides.

📝 Full Traceability

Original patient input, AI output, and any doctor edits are all preserved together on the case record — nothing is silently overwritten.

06 · Case Assignment

Getting the right case to the right doctor

Superadmins can hand a case directly to a named clinician, with instant, dual-channel notification.

01
One-click assignment
From the Cases list, a superadmin picks any active doctor from a dropdown to take ownership of a case.
02
Instant email notification
The assigned doctor receives a branded email — "New Case Assigned to You" — with the patient profile and a direct link to open the case.
03
In-app toast notification
The moment the doctor is next in the app, a clickable toast appears — "New Case Assigned to you →" — taking them straight to the case.
04
Full assignment history
Every case retains who assigned it, to whom, and when — visible in both the case record and the audit log.
07 · Notifications

Targeted, not noisy

Notifications are scoped precisely to the people who need to act — never a blanket broadcast to the entire organization.

New case submitted

Only the one superadmin who manages that doctor's account is notified — not every administrator in the system.

Case approved or denied

The case owner and the assigned doctor are notified — the people directly involved in that patient's care.

Case assigned to you

The newly assigned doctor is notified by both email and an in-app toast — the reviewing superadmin is not re-notified.

Personalized, localized emails

Every email uses the recipient's real profile photo and shows timestamps in their timezone, not the server's.

✉️ Branded HTML emails 🔔 Real-time in-app toasts 🎯 Recipient-scoped delivery 🌍 Timezone-aware timestamps
08 · Team Channels

Secure messaging built for clinical teams

A dedicated communication layer so care teams never have to leave the platform to coordinate.

👑 Head-Doctor-led channels

Superadmins create a channel and assign a Head Doctor, who can invite other clinicians to join.

📜 Channel rules

Each channel carries its own code of conduct, visible to every member at any time.

🚨 Admin call escalation

Any doctor can summon a superadmin directly into a channel for urgent situations — tracked in a dedicated Admin Section.

🛠️ Moderation tools

Superadmins can warn, timeout, kick, or ban members, and manage rules — all via simple in-chat commands.

10 · Safety & Emergency Handling

Nothing urgent gets lost in a queue

The platform is designed so that critical cases are visually and operationally impossible to miss.

🆘

Emergency detection

Cases flagged as an emergency are surfaced with a distinct badge and route straight to the responsible superadmin.

⚠️

Urgent review flag

Borderline cases that need faster attention are marked "requires urgent review" rather than sitting in the standard queue.

📣

Adverse event alerts

Any detected adverse event triggers an immediate, dedicated notification — separate from routine case updates.

11 · Compliance & Audit

Every action, permanently accountable

Vianova Health is built around the assumption that any clinical or administrative action may need to be reviewed later.

  • Full audit log of every case action — created, assigned, reviewed, approved, denied
  • Consent tracking with expiry alerts before access lapses
  • User deactivation events broadcast for organizational awareness
  • Break-glass access events specifically logged and surfaced
  • Ownership model — every case always has a clear responsible party
  • Immutable timestamps for every recorded event
  • Role-based access control enforced on every API endpoint
  • Dedicated Audit & Compliance dashboard for organization-wide review
